Thomas Dam

The man who made trolls

Here is a time line of Thomas Dams life:

  • 1976 - Thomas Dam creates the first Troll doll, "Dam Things" in Denmark.
  • 1991 - The Troll doll phenomenon gains international popularity, leading to the creation of a dedicated company, "Dam Things" by Thomas Dam.
  • 2003 - Thomas Dam sells the rights to the Troll dolls to the Danish toy company, Lego.
  • 2009 - Thomas Dam passes away, leaving behind a lasting legacy with his iconic creation.
  • 2013 - DreamWorks Animation releases the animated film "Trolls," bringing the Troll dolls back into the mainstream.
  • 2020 - The success of "Trolls World Tour" solidifies the enduring popularity of the Troll dolls, further expanding the franchise.
