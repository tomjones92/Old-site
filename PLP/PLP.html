Monchhchi

The Monchhichi, originally created in Japan in 1974, has become a beloved worldwide sensation, enchanting generations with its adorable design and endearing charm.

A normal Monchhichi plush toy typically measures approximately 20 centimeters (8 inches) in height from head to toe. However, please note that there may be variations in size based on different Monchhichi models or editions.
